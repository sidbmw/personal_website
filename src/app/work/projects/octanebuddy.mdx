Never overpay for fuel or charging again! OctaneBuddy is your intelligent companion for finding the best fuel prices and EV charging stations near you. With real-time updates and seamless CarPlay integration, saving money and finding spots has never been easier.

**Key Features:**

*   **Smart Price Comparison:** Instantly compare prices at nearby stations with automatic updates.
*   **Multi-Country Support:** Provides fuel prices (Regular, Medium, Premium, Diesel, E85, etc.) for USA, Canada, UK, Germany, Austria, and Australia.
*   **EV Charging (Pro):** Find Level 2, DC Fast Charging, Tesla stations, and more, with real-time availability, connector types (J1772, CCS, CHAdeMO, Tesla), power info, and pricing.
*   **Interactive Map:** Visualize stations with color-coded pricing.
*   **Flexible Search & Filters:** Customize search radius, filter by brand, and find Top Tier™ certified fuel.
*   **Turn-by-Turn Navigation:** Get precise directions with real-time traffic.
*   **Premium CarPlay Experience:** Full integration for safe, hands-free operation while driving.
*   **Privacy Focused:** Requires location access only to find nearby stations; no user data is collected.

Iteratively improved through TestFlight releases, incorporating user feedback and growing to over 1600 users.
